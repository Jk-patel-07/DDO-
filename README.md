## About DDO

DDO is a modern desktop tool designed to reduce the need to open many separate apps, pages, and services.

It combines everyday tools into one clean interface with a compact top status bar, small popups, AI support, company features, communication tools, media controls, productivity options, and system shortcuts.

The main goal of DDO is to create a simple, fast, and intelligent desktop experience.

---

## Why DDO?

Users often switch between many applications for simple tasks such as searching, chatting with AI, opening music, checking notifications, changing settings, or accessing company tools.

DDO brings these actions together in one place.

### Main Benefits

- Faster access to daily tools
- Fewer full-screen windows
- Clean popup-based interface
- Multiple AI services in one place
- Quick access to local and online apps
- Company and employee tools
- Built-in productivity features
- Modern desktop experience

### DDO One Company Form

DDO One is the company registration and onboarding form.

#### Form Information

- Company name

- City

- PIN code
- Applicant name
- Applicant email
- Applicant phone number
- Position in company
- Company logo
- Company photos
- Company registration proof
- Additional optional details

#### Company Flow

1. A company completes the DDO One form.
2. The company submits its details.
3. The application is reviewed.
4. The request is approved or rejected.
5. Approved companies receive login details.
6. The company opens its own DDO dashboard.
